Hello there!
I was born and raised in Budapest, Hungary. I grew up with a deep passion for computers and rowing. I still remember the first computer my family got from a friend because we could not afford one. My father had to work 7 days a week to provide for us. Screen time was a family time for my 4 brothers and I. We were always so excited to discover the secrets of computers which developed in me a life-long passion for software.
I moved to Rexburg, Idaho to study Software-Engineering at BYU-Idaho and I have been falling in love with the nature and wild-life around here. I like to spend time in the mountains in every season whether I am on a pair of skis or on feet. I also started attending country dances where I met the love of my life and we got married after 9 months of dating.
